# aoc_online

A simulator for [Advent of Code](https://adventofcode.com/2025) days 1-4. (ignore days 5-7). First look at the respective days on that site.

## Day 1
Put 'R' or 'L' followed by a number in the textfield, then press enter
## Day 2
Put an ID range in the textfield, then press enter. It will tell you which IDs in that range are invalid.
## Day 3
Put a row of joltage ratings in the textfield, then press enter. It will tell you what the maximum joltage is.
## Day 4
The grey area is a 10x10 grid. Click on a square to put a toilet paper roll there (or remove one), and press the checkbox to have the forklifts pick up the toilet paper rolls they can.
